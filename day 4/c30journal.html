Welcome to IJSSE

The International Journal of Scientific and Social Excellence (IJSSE) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal aimed at disseminating high-quality research in various fields of science and social studies. We provide a platform for authors, reviewers, and editors to collaborate effectively in advancing academic knowledge.

About Journal

IJSSE is dedicated to publishing high-impact research and scholarly articles that contribute to scientific and social excellence. Our journal covers a wide range of topics in the fields of science and social studies. ISSN: 2395-1443 (Print) | ISSN: 2395-1451 (Online)

Publication Ethics

Our publication process adheres to the highest ethical standards, ensuring the integrity of the research published. We follow best practices in peer review, conflict of interest disclosure, and publication transparency.
