Administrator Features

Login

Admins log in securely to access system functionalities.

Manage Garbage Bins

Admins can add, update, or delete garbage bins in the system.

Assign Routes

Admins assign the most efficient routes for drivers to ensure quick collection.

View Reports

Admins can access and analyze garbage collection reports.

General Public Features

Register & Login

The public can create an account and log in to track their complaints.

Register Complaints

Users can report any issues with garbage collection or disposal.

Track Complaints

Users can track the status of their complaints and receive updates on progress.

Driver Features

Login

Drivers log in to view their tasks for the day.

Choose Routes

Drivers can view and select the best routes for garbage collection.

Update Garbage Load

Drivers can update the system with the amount of garbage collected during the route.
